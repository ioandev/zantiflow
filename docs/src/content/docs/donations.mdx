zantiflow is free and open source, with **no paid checkout**. There are two ways PRO happens and one
way to support the project.

## Support the project

If zantiflow is useful to you, please support it via **GitHub Sponsors**. Donations fund the open-source
work; they are **support-only** and don't buy anything.

## Getting PRO

PRO is **promo-code only**. A fresh code is auto-generated on the homepage every couple of weeks and
grants a month of PRO (extendable, capped at ~60 days). Sign in, redeem it on the dashboard — done. No
admin, no billing.

PRO unlocks tighter attention thresholds (≥1 min vs ≥5 min) and chat-DM notifications (Discord +
Telegram) on top of the Web Push everyone gets.

## Self-hosting

Everything is open and self-hostable — `docker compose up` with your own Google OAuth app and VAPID
keys. See [Backend](/backend/) and the `deploy/` directory.
